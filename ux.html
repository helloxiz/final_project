Background

In 2020, people across the globe experienced a rapid shift to remote work that upended their existing work practices.

Over two years later, some are returning to the workplace and work practices are shifting once again – this time from remote to hybrid.

Hybrid work, a mix of remote and face-to-face work, will become more and more the norm for companies in many industries.

Problem Statement

The breakout of Covid-19 has changed people’s lifestyles completely on a global scale. In particular, people’s working styles have completely changed. However, Although remote working effectively protects the health of the workers, it leaves a lot of workspaces unoccupied, which becomes a waste of cost for companies. Since hybrid working is a big trend affecting the future of work, how might we help companies build a flexible and attractive workspace through an efficient booking and scheduling system?

How might we help future companies to build a flexible and attractive workspace and an efficient scheduling meeting system within a hybrid working environment?

Solution

To help companies better organize their workspace and manage employees under the hybrid working mode, we designed an application named “Meetspace”. Meetspace empowers employees to book desks and spaces whenever they intend to work at the physical office. In addition, it implements a powerful scheduling system that helps teams find time slots that work for each team member. Meetspace holds the mission to provide a seamless hybrid working experience for companies and their employees.
